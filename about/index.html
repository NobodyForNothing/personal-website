About

Hey there! I'm DerDilla, a self-proclaimed hobbyist developer from Germany.

So, what inspired me to create this website? Well, there are two main reasons. Firstly, I found myself with an abundance of time and a burning desire to delve deeper into web development. This led me to embark on the exciting journey of creating my own corner of the internet.

Secondly, I think that individuals should have control over their digital presence, free from reliance on big social media companies. I encourage everyone to build their own websites and shape their online identities according to their own vision.

If you're curious about my projects, be sure to check out the dedicated section below. There, you'll find a showcase of my endeavors and the exciting challenges I've taken on.
